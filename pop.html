Olivia Rodrigo

All About Olivia

Born: February 20, 2003, age 18

Genre(s): Pop

Labels: Interscope and Geffen Records


Olivia Rodrigo is an actress, singer, and songwriter who has been featured in multiple Disney shows including her recent Disney+ show, High School Musical: The Musical: The Series. She rose to fame through her recent singles that she has put out through her show and at the beginning of 2021. Her three debut singles broke multiple streaming and Billboard records soon after their release. In addition, her debut album release, SOUR, has already reached the top charts that includes her debut single drivers liscense. Olivia has already performed on live on a big stage at the Brit awards and on SNL before her album was even released. Currently, her album is rising in the charts and the second season of her hit show, High School Musical: The Musical: The Series, is streaming.
